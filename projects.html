01
Telecom · Predictive Analytics Complete

Customer Churn Prediction

End-to-end churn pipeline on IBM Telco data (7,043 customers, 26.5% churn rate) — SMOTE imbalance handling, XGBoost modelling, and a SHAP explainability layer that translates each prediction into a specific driver so the retention team knows exactly what to target.

Outcome — XGBoost achieved AUC-ROC 0.837 and 76% churn recall vs. 63% for the logistic baseline. Contract type (SHAP 0.596), tenure (0.515), and fibre internet (0.406) were the top three drivers. Targeting the highest-risk 20% with a $10/month retention offer is projected to generate ~$52K net benefit annually — a 7.4× ROI.
