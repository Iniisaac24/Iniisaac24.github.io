Project 06 · Real Estate · Geospatial

Property Price Prediction
& Mapping

In progress

Predicting King County home prices and mapping where the market is mispriced — pairing a regression model with an interactive map of under- and over-valued areas.

Planned Approach

What this project will cover

01

Clean & engineer

Prepare King County housing data and engineer location, size, and condition features.

02

Model

XGBoost regression with cross-validated tuning, evaluated on a held-out set.

03

Find mispricing

Compare predicted vs. actual prices to flag consistently under- or over-valued neighbourhoods.

04

Map it

Render an interactive folium map so the pricing gaps are visible at a glance.
