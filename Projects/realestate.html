Findings

Location is the whole story

XGBoost explains 91% of price variance (MAE ~$63k), a huge jump over the linear baseline (0.61) — which can't capture the non-linear location premium.

Latitude is the single most important feature (permutation importance 0.43), ahead of living area, distance to downtown Seattle, and construction grade. In King County, where beats what.

The model flags mispriced ZIPs: 98014 (+11.9%), 98168 (+7.7%) and 98166 (+7.1%) sell consistently below model value — a value-investing shortlist — while 98070 (−8.0%) sells above.
