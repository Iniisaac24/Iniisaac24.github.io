Investment Portfolio
Risk Dashboard

In progress

A Streamlit dashboard that quantifies portfolio risk from live market data and benchmarks it against the S&P 500 — Sharpe Ratio, Value at Risk, Max Drawdown, and sector correlation in one view.

Planned Approach

What this project will cover

01

Live data

Pull daily prices for ~15 holdings via yfinance and compute a clean returns series.

02

Risk metrics

Sharpe Ratio, 95% historical VaR, and Maximum Drawdown, each benchmarked against the S&P 500.

03

Concentration risk

A sector correlation heatmap to surface holdings that move together and undermine diversification.

04

Ship it

Package as an interactive Streamlit app — this page will show screenshots plus a launch link once it is deployed.
