Project 05 · HR · People Analytics

Employee Attrition Analysis

In progress

Identifying flight-risk employees and the drivers behind attrition on the IBM HR dataset — turning people data into interventions HR can actually act on.

Planned Approach

What this project will cover

01

Exploratory analysis

Examine attrition against satisfaction, overtime, tenure, and role to surface patterns.

02

Flight-risk model

A Random Forest to score each employee's probability of leaving, evaluated on recall for the leavers.

03

Segmentation

K-Means clustering into interpretable risk profiles for targeted retention.

04

Recommendations

Summarise into HR policy recommendations, supported by a Tableau Public view.
