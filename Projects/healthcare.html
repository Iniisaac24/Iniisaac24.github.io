Project 02 · Healthcare · Statistical Analysis

Hospital Readmission Analysis

In progress

Investigating which clinical factors drive 30-day hospital readmissions in the Diabetes 130-US dataset (100,000+ encounters) — and validating them with proper statistical testing rather than eyeballing charts.

Planned Approach

What this project will cover

01

Profile & clean

Load the Diabetes 130-US dataset, handle missing and placeholder-coded values, and consolidate diagnosis codes into clinical groups.

02

Exploratory analysis

Break down 30-day readmission rates by diagnosis group, age band, and prior utilisation to form hypotheses.

03

Statistical testing

Chi-square tests for categorical associations and Mann-Whitney U for skewed numeric variables, to separate real signal from noise.

04

Recommendations

Translate statistically significant findings into concrete clinical recommendations.
