Project 04 · Retail · Time Series

Walmart Sales Forecasting

In progress

A 12-week demand forecast across 45 stores, comparing a classical baseline against gradient-boosted models — the kind of forecast that drives inventory and staffing decisions.

Planned Approach

What this project will cover

01

Assemble the series

Build weekly sales per store with holiday flags and markdown features.

02

Classical baseline

SARIMA for a transparent baseline, then Prophet for automatic holiday and seasonality handling.

03

Gradient-boosted challenger

Engineer lag and rolling-window features for a LightGBM model to beat the baseline.

04

Honest evaluation

Compare all three on a held-out 12-week window using MAPE, not in-sample fit.
