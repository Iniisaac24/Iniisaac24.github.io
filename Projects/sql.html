Findings

What the database says about the business

It's a one-and-done marketplace. Only 3.12% of 96,096 customers ever place a second order — retention, not acquisition, is the untapped lever.

Late delivery is a review killer. On-time orders average 4.29★; late orders collapse to 2.57★, with 54% scoring 1–2 stars (vs 9% on-time). Logistics is the customer-experience strategy.

Revenue is highly concentrated. São Paulo alone drives 38.3% of national revenue; the top three states (SP, RJ, MG) account for 63%.

Payments are card-led (74%) but boleto (bank slip) remains 19% — a distinctly Brazilian channel worth designing for.
